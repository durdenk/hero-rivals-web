Odyssey Arena Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Odyssey Arena ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our Android game.

1. Information We Collect

Odyssey Arena does not require account registration and does not ask you to provide personal information to play the game.

The app may store gameplay-related data locally on your device, such as preferences, sound settings, progress, unlocked content, currency, or other in-game state.

2. How We Use Information

Local gameplay data is used only to operate game features, remember your preferences, and provide a better play experience.

3. Local Data Storage

Gameplay data and preferences are stored on your device. This data is generally removed when you uninstall the app, depending on your device and Android backup settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Odyssey Arena may be distributed through Google Play. Google Play may collect information such as install, device, crash, and diagnostic data according to Google's own policies.

You can review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade personal information. We only share information if required by law, needed to protect our rights, or handled by third-party platform services such as Google Play according to their policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Odyssey Arena is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information associated with the app. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
